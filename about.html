About Us

My Story

Baby Binky Boutique was fittingly created by a mother of twins. Raising two boys, it’s not always easy keeping track of what goes into their mouths. A big part of that was their pacifiers! They often fell to the ground repeatedly and unfortunately I wasn’t always there to catch them in time for a rinse. I thought, “what if I could eliminate the chance of them falling to the ground all together?” That’s when I got the idea to make customizable pacifier clip! It was a simple fun solution which could prevent dust and germs from going into the mouths of our little ones.


It was a simple fun solution which could prevent dust and germs from going into the mouths of our little ones. I personally take the time to make each and every one of them byhand. Rest assured, it’s one less thing parents need to worry about!
